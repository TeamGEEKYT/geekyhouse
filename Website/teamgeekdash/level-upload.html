LEVEL UPLOAD

This page will explain everything about Level Upload.


LEVEL UPLOAD

Level upload on itself requires a few things. You can upload a level under following conditions:

- Having an account

- Not posting anything innapropriate (Includes NSFW, harmful content, swears, etc) with no warning screen in-level (and confirmation upon clicking)

- Level is humanly possible on Normal mode, verified or not

- Level is not a copy-paste of another one's in the GDPS


LEVEL RATING

Your level can be rated if:

- The level duration is at least 30 seconds (Must last 31 seconds). On platformer levels, if the level cannot be speedrunned in less than 30 seconds, the level can be rated. If required, toggle off the End Trigger and toggle it back on 30 seconds later.

- The level doesn't contains stolen stuff from Geometry Dash or the GDPS with no difference without permission.


To get Creator Points with your level, you must verify the level, or let anyone else do it for you, with video proof if the level is Insane or Demon. Upon Team Geek validation, you get the required amount of CP

Same goes with Featured, Epic, Legendary and Mythic rates.

APPEARANCE IN DEMONLIST

For your level to appear in the Demonlist, the level must:

- Be a Rated Insane Demon or a Rated Extreme Demon

- Be verified with video proof from yourself or someone else

- Be hard enough to appear in the Demonlist
