Known Issues and bugs

This page lists all unpatched and known bugs and issues from Find The Cars: Modern Edition either reported by our players in our Discord Server, either discovered by ourselves.

This is a weird bug where you can only mute the game music or hide the UI, but you can't do both.

Issue has been found and will be patched on a future update.


Grab&Go music while outside of it

If you stick on a Grab&Go wall, the music might randomly stop and trigger the In-shop-music. Issue has been found and will be patched on a future update.


Donation button does not work anymore


Invisible Wall in Impossible Secret Obby

Issue has been found and will be solved on a future update.
