Livestream Rules

On my channel, as long as you are nice and follow YouTube TOS, you should be fine.

Yet, remembering you some points shouldn't hurt you.


Liberty of speech

You can talk about anything you want, as long as:

- You don't hurt or mean to someone or me

- You are not political

- You don't advertise yourself without my permission


Ban Bypassing

Bypassing a ban is an Instant Perm Ban onto your alt account, and a longer ban period will be applied if it was not an permanent one.


Raiding

Feel free to raid me on Twitch.

However, don't raid me if you just want to ruin the Livestream.

The streamer that raided me won't be punished if the raid wasn't meant to ruin the livestream.


Spam

Feel free to spam at important parts... But don't when it is just me talking to you or when a random, yet non-interesting event happens.


Moderation

The moderation team has to mute/ban users that breaks the rules.

If a moderator punishes someone that does not break the rule or that does not punish someone who breaks them might be unmodded if this was done intentionaly.
